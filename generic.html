Beyond the data sets and analytics, my life is a canvas of experiences. Here's a glimpse:

Embracing the Simple Joys

Whether it's tending to my garden or getting hands-on in the fields, I find joy in the simple pleasures of working with the earth. It's a grounding contrast to the digital realms I often navigate.

Travel: Exploring Earth's Marvels

From the mystic moai statues of Easter Island to the reflective expanse of Bolivia's Salar de Uyuni, I cherish the moments spent in awe of our world's wonders. Traveling fuels my creativity and reminds me that there's always more to learn and explore.

Through the Lens

Capturing moments through my lens, I see the world from different perspectives. The vibrant glass art at Seattle's Chihuly Garden and Glass or the serene beauty of flamingos at Eduardo Avaroa Andean Fauna National Reserve in Bolivia - each photo tells a story.

A Lifelong Learner

Off the clock, I dive into books that fuel my passion for data and personal growth. From "Storytelling with Data" to "Lean Analytics," and even words of wisdom from Michelle Obama, I'm always seeking knowledge that sharpens my mind and inspires my work.
